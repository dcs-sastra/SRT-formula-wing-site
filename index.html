ABOUT US

SASTRA Racing Team is the official automobile racing team of SASTRA Deemed University, Thanjavur. The Formula Student wing, a team of about 45 passionate automobile enthusiasts, aims at designing and building Formula-style open-wheel race cars from ground up and competing at national-level competitions like Formula Bharat, ESVC, and Supra SAE. After two successful years at Formula Bharat Virtuals and after securing the 2nd place as a rookie team in FB 2022, the team has now set its sights for the 2023 season with a zeal to win.

Structural Rigidity of the car during high-speed cornering is very important. Chassis helps to keep the car intact while pulling high G' forces. It's the skeleton that puts all the components in place and in shape. The primary goal of the chassis is for the body to withstand the worst of forces to keep your driver safe. Aerodynamics, on the other hand plays a key role especially in formula style cars. The main focus of the distinctive aero structures on a formula style car is to reduce drag and increase downforce on the car. This helps in protecting crucial components like radiator and maximizing the grip of the tyres and helps us breeze past our competitors!

It is the primary subsystem that dictates the entire “feel” of the car. The primary goal of the suspension system is to maintain adequate grip for optimum handling, and to provide a reasonable “ride”. It takes coordinated efforts from all the subsystems to build a suspension that achieves the deceptively simple objectives of ride and handling. A good suspension, dampens vibrations, maintains grip and influences the confidence with which a driver can push the car to its limits.

Steering, If this doesn't work, your car is as good as a really fast Wagon. It is one if the most crucial system with which the driver can use to Maneuver the car voluntarily.It helps in overall stability of the vehicle. Maneuvering the vehicle at the optimum angle with the least tire wear is the primary goal of the subsystem. The design is effective only when the driver feels safe with the amount of control they have over the steering.

Quite an important subsystem that is the beating heart of the car. The selection of the power unit along with the transmission influences the amount of "vroom" that can be extracted from the engine. It focuses on selecting an appropriate engine coherent with market availability, making it work seamlessly in the car with a good design of systems like intake, exhaust, and transmission. The powertrain team makes sure that all of the systems work in synergy and evaluates the safety and performance of the components involved by conducting a wide range of analysis from CFD to structural.
