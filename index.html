FORMULA WING

ABOUT US

SASTRA Racing Team is the official automobile racing team of SASTRA Deemed University, Thanjavur. The Formula Student wing, a team of about 45 passionate automobile enthusiasts, aims at designing and building Formula-style open-wheel race cars from ground up and competing at national-level competitions like Formula Bharat, ESVC, and Supra SAE. After two successful years at Formula Bharat Virtuals and after securing the 2nd place as a rookie team in FB 2022, the team has now set its sights for the 2023 season with a zeal to win.
